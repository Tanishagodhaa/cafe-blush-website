## üìã Managing Menu Items

### Adding a New Menu Item

1. Click **"Menu Items"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"New Menu Item"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: Item name (e.g., "Nutella Shake")
   - **Description**: Short description
   - **Price**: Price in ‚Çπ (just the number, e.g., 130)
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown
   - **Dietary Tags**: Check all that apply (Veg, Egg, Dairy)
   - **Image**: Upload a photo (optional but recommended)
   - **Featured**: Check to show on homepage
   - **Available**: Uncheck to hide from menu
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Editing an Existing Item

1. Click **"Menu Items"**
2. Click on the item you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Hiding an Item (Without Deleting)

1. Edit the item
2. Uncheck **"Available"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

## üéâ Managing Offers & Deals

### Creating a New Offer

1. Click **"Offers & Deals"**
2. Click **"New Offer"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Offer name (e.g., "Winter Combo")
   - **Description**: What's included
   - **Image**: Upload an attractive image
   - **Valid From**: Start date
   - **Valid Until**: End date
   - **Active**: Check to show on website
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Ending an Offer Early

1. Edit the offer
2. Uncheck **"Active"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

---

## üñºÔ∏è Managing Gallery

### Adding Gallery Images

1. Click **"Gallery Images"**
2. Click **"New Gallery Image"**
3. Upload your image
4. Add alt text (describe the image)
5. Add caption (optional)
6. Set order number (lower = appears first)
7. Click **"Publish"**

### Best Practices for Images

- Use high-quality, well-lit photos
- Ideal size: 1200x800 pixels
- Show food close-ups, cafe interior, happy customers
- Keep file size under 500KB

## ‚≠ê Managing Reviews

### Adding a Customer Review

1. Click **"Customer Reviews"**
2. Click **"New Review"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Customer Name**: First name only is fine
   - **Rating**: 1-5 stars
   - **Comment**: Their feedback
   - **Date**: When they gave the review
   - **Source**: Where the review came from
4. Click **"Publish"**
